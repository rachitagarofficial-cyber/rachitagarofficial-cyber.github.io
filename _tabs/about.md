### We are a group of college friends who believe India deserves better.

What started as late-night debates in hostel rooms and passionate discussions over chai has now become **Reform India** — a platform dedicated to writing honest, thoughtful, and impactful blogs on social and political issues that affect the lives of everyday Indians.

We are not professional journalists or policy experts. We are simply a small group of college students and recent graduates who care deeply about our country. Coming from different states, backgrounds, and viewpoints, we are united by one goal:

**To make complex social and political issues accessible, understandable, and actionable for the common citizen.**

### Why We Write

Most Indians feel frustrated with the system but don’t always have the time or clear information to understand the root causes. Mainstream media is often biased or sensationalized. We wanted to change that.

At **Reform India**, we break down important topics — education, healthcare, governance, inequality, judiciary, electoral reforms, and pressing cultural issues — in clear and straightforward language. We don’t just criticize problems; we try to explain **why** they exist and **what** can actually be done about them.

### Our Mission

- Inform the general public with facts and proper context  
- Spark honest and meaningful conversations  
- Encourage young Indians to think critically  
- Inspire action towards positive, ground-level change in India  

---

### Who We Are

We are a tight-knit group of college friends — engineers, humanities students, law aspirants, economists, and writers. Some of us are still in college, while others have just graduated.

**This entire website is created, written, and maintained solely by us.**

There is no big team, no office, and no external funding. Every article you read here is researched and written by us in our free time — between lectures, assignments, exams, and internships. The website design, content, and updates are also handled entirely by our small group.

It is 100% passion-driven.

### Our Promise

- No political affiliations or hidden agendas  
- No sensational clickbait  
- Well-researched and intellectually honest writing  
- Content written for the common Indian, not just the elite  

We don’t claim to know everything. We make mistakes, we learn, and we keep improving. What we promise is sincerity and honesty in every piece.
