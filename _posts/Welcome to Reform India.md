# Welcome to Reform India

Hello and welcome to Reform India! This is a blogging website designed by and for the people of India.

We strive to:
* Bring about social and political reforms in India
* Make the general public aware of government policies and how it affects them
* Expose any wrongdoings pan India

## Our motto
> "Political tyranny is nothing compared to social tyranny and a reformer who defies society is a more courageous man than a politician who defies government."
<br><br>
~ Dr. B.R. Ambedkar
